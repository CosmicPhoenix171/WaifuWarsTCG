## Hero Highlights

### Emotion-Driven Combat
- Bonds fuel confessions, meltdowns, and clutch comebacks.
- Mood states escalate from **Neutral → Dere → Extreme** with cinematic flair.
- Bond Cards weave relationships that reshape the battlefield mid-match.

### Deck Crafting, Simplified
- Build 20-card decks that mix Waifu, Support, and Trap cards for explosive synergies.
- Unlock faction bonuses with 10+ cards from a single dere archetype.
- Hybrid archetypes let you tailor counters to the meta while keeping your signature style.

### Collect, Trade, Flex
- Rip open animated booster packs with generous rarity glows and spark showers.
- Trade securely in the global hub with offer tracking, filters, and expirations.
- Track collection milestones and badge out your legendary pulls.

## The Waifu Wars Difference

- **Rock-Paper-Scissors, reimagined**: Passion Surge vs. Stoic Control vs. Heart Guard keeps matchups readable without hard-locks.
- **Narrative-driven mechanics**: Mood swings, confessions, and last-episode power-ups punctuate each duel.
- **Universal accessibility**: Play seamlessly on touch devices with adaptive layouts and scalable card UI.
